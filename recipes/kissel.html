Fruit Pudding (Kissell)

This is a version of a traditional Scandinavian recipe. The original calls for raspberries, but you can use any kind of soft fruit you can get. This will serve about 6-8 people.

Ingredients

  • 2lb. or so of soft fruit
  • 1 pint. cider
  • 4-6 oz. honey
  • a few sprigs and seed heads of Sweet Cicely, chopped
  • buttermilk or yoghurt

Steps

  1. Put the cider and honey in a pan and bring to the boil. Simmer briskly for about 5-10 minutes, until reduced by about a third.
  2. Move the pan to a cooler part of the fire and add the fruit and Sweet Cicely.
  3. Cook gently until the fruit is tender.
  4. Allow the fruit to cool (covered, out of the sun), then break it up with a wooden spoon.
  5. Serve warm or cold, with buttermilk or yoghurt.
