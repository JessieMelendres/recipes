Carrot Casserole

This Carrot Casserole is a great side dish for Easter or any holiday celebration.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 2/3 Cup of pearl barley
  • 1 to 1 1/2 Cups of milk
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sea Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of honey
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 2 to 3 Tablespoons of bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and add barley. Partially cover and simmer until water is absorbed.
  2. Add 1 cup of milk and simmer until absorbed. If the barley is not done add more milk - done but firm with some milk left , cool a little.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  4. Mix carrots, salt, honey and nutmeg into milk and barley then add eggs.
  5. Butter a 1 1/2 quart casserole with half the butter and sprinkle in part of the bread crumbs and then pour in the mixture, topping with the rest of the bread crumbs and dot with the butter.
  6. Bake 40 minutes or until top and sides are brown.
